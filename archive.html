BENGKEL DAN PERSEMBAHAN BONEKA

Date: 27 February 2020 - 13 March 2020

Venue: National Museum

NATIONAL HERITAGE ARTEFACT

This malay belt buckle is made of solid gold. The main body of the buckle is embossed with a design of plaited gold wire and granules. The motif is of a lotus flower and is studded with a variety of coloured gems. Originated from Kelantan. This artefact is declared as Tangible Heritage Object under the National Heritage List 2009.
