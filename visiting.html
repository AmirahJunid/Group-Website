HOW TO GO TO NATIONAL MUSEUM OF MALAYSIA?

Four ways or method you can use in order to visit National Museum. You can use public bus, MRT, Putra LRT or using your own cars.

PUBLIC BUS

RapidKL

MRT

You can stop at National Museum stations

Putra LRT

From KL Sentral stations you just need to walk for about 0.5 km before arive at National Museum

OWN CARS

You can go to National Museum by your own car. Car Parking was provide by National Museum. You just need to pay the fee according to Museum Negara rules and regulation. Rm 3.00 for the frist hour and adding Rm 3.00 for the next hour. For the bus, van and taxi, they will charge as much as RM10.00. If you missing your parking ticket, you need to pay RM 50.00 for the charged.
